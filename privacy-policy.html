Introduction

Welcome to Invoice Maker ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains our data practices regarding our iOS mobile application (the "App"). We want to be completely transparent: Invoice Maker does not collect, store, transmit, or use any personal data or user information whatsoever.

This policy describes how our app operates entirely on your device without any data collection, tracking, or external communication.

No Data Collection

Invoice Maker is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any information from you, including:

  • Personal Information: No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact details
  • Invoice Data: Your invoices, client information, and business data stay entirely on your device
  • Device Information: No device identifiers, operating system details, or hardware information
  • Usage Data: No analytics, tracking, or monitoring of how you use the app
  • Location Data: No access to or collection of location information
  • Crash Reports: No automatic crash reporting or error logging

Local Data Storage Only

All data you create in Invoice Maker is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This includes:

  • Invoice information and templates
  • Client contact details
  • Business information and logos
  • App settings and preferences

Important: Since data is stored locally only, it will be lost if you delete the app or reset your device. We recommend regularly backing up important invoices by exporting them as PDFs.

No Internet Connection Required

Invoice Maker's core functionality works completely offline and does not require an internet connection. The app does not:

  • Connect to our servers for app functionality
  • Send invoice or business data over the internet
  • Use cloud storage or synchronization for your invoices
  • Communicate with third-party services for app features
  • Download updates or content from the web during normal use

Exception: Internet connection is only required for payment processing when purchasing the app or premium features through the App Store or our payment processors.

Third-Party Services

Invoice Maker minimizes third-party integrations to protect your privacy. We only use essential services:

  • Payment Processing: Apple App Store and/or third-party payment processors for app purchases and subscriptions

We do NOT use any of the following services:

  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integration
  • No cloud storage services
  • No customer support platforms
  • No user tracking or behavioral analysis

Payment Processing

When you make purchases within the app or subscribe to premium features:

  • Apple App Store: In-app purchases are processed by Apple. Apple handles all payment information according to their privacy policy
  • Transaction Data: Payment processors may collect transaction information necessary to process payments
  • No Storage: We do not store your payment information on our systems

Important: Payment processing is handled entirely by third-party services. Your payment information is never stored locally on your device or on our servers. Please review the privacy policies of Apple App Store and any payment processors used.

Data Security

Since Invoice Maker does not collect or transmit any data, there are no security risks related to data breaches or unauthorized access to your information on external servers. Your data security is ensured by:

  • Local storage only - no data leaves your device
  • No network connections or data transmission
  • No external servers or databases
  • Standard iOS app sandboxing protections

Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, traditional privacy rights related to data access, correction, or deletion don't apply. However, you have complete control over your data:

  • Complete Data Control: All your data remains on your device under your control
  • Easy Data Deletion: Simply delete the app to remove all associated data
  • No Data Requests Needed: No need to contact us for data access or deletion
  • Export Your Data: Use the app's PDF export feature to backup your invoices

Children's Privacy

Since Invoice Maker does not collect any personal information or data from users of any age, there are no special privacy concerns related to children's use of the app. The app is safe for users of all ages as no data collection occurs.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Since we don't collect data, changes will typically be minor and related to clarifications or legal updates. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
